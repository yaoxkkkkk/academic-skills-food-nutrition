# Ethics & Integrity Checklist

Canonical checklist for `food-review`'s `reviewer_integrity` and `food-deep-research`'s
`ethics_reviewer`. Each item: pass / flag (with severity + required fix).

## Research integrity
- [ ] **Citation integrity:** every in-text citation resolves to a real, non-retracted source; no fabricated references; sources represented faithfully (no over-generalization beyond the tested matrix/dose/population).
- [ ] **Data integrity:** numbers in text match tables/figures; no image manipulation signals (duplicated/spliced gels, blots, micrographs); implausible precision flagged.
- [ ] **No plagiarism:** original prose, not close paraphrase of a single source; quotations attributed.
- [ ] **Statistics honest:** no p-hacking/HARKing; non-significant results reported; multiple comparisons handled.

## Human & animal ethics (food/nutrition)
- [ ] **Human studies** (sensory panels, dietary trials): ethics-committee approval, informed consent, panel size/screening reported; trial registration where applicable.
- [ ] **Animal studies:** approval + welfare (ARRIVE 2.0).
- [ ] **Allergens & food safety:** allergen disclosure; no unsafe or actionable harmful guidance; pathogen/biosafety handling appropriate.

## Disclosure
- [ ] **Competing interests & funding** declared; material COIs of key cited studies noted where they bear on conclusions.
- [ ] **Author contributions** (CRediT); **data/code availability** statement.
- [ ] **AI-assistance disclosure** present and honest.

## Verdict
Overall: **clear · fix-then-clear · blocked**. Critical/high flags (fabrication,
missing human-ethics approval, unsafe guidance) **block** and must be resolved
before submission/finalization.
